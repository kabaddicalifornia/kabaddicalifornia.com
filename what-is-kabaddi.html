Learn the Game

What is Kabaddi?

Kabaddi is a high-intensity combative sport that combines strength, speed, wrestling, stamina, and strategy.

Circle Style Kabaddi

Circle style Kabaddi, also known as Punjab-style Kabaddi, is one of the most traditional and exciting forms of the sport. It is physical, fast, and deeply connected to Punjabi culture.

Why It Matters

Kabaddi is more than a game. It represents pride, discipline, brotherhood, toughness, and respect for where we come from.

From Punjab to California

Punjabi communities brought Kabaddi to countries around the world, including Canada, the United Kingdom, Australia, and the United States. In California, the sport continues to grow through tournaments, clubs, and community support.

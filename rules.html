Rules & Positions

Circle Style Kabaddi Rules

A simple guide for fans who are new to the sport.

Basic Rules

  • Two teams compete against each other.
  • A raider enters the opponent’s side to score.
  • Stoppers try to tackle and stop the raider.
  • The raider must return safely to earn a point.
  • Matches are fast, physical, and full of momentum swings.

Main Positions

  • Raider: The offensive player who attacks.
  • Stopper: The defensive player who tackles.
  • Corner: Support defender who helps trap the raider.
  • All-Rounder: A player who can raid and defend.
